The Artist

Ocean Opera are honoured that the multitalented Montreal artist Laura Mesones has kindly agreed to work with us on the production as visual aesthetics lead and Live Painter!

Laura Mesones has kindly agreed to give one of her beautiful Live Painting performances to introduce our free for Fringe volunteers show on Wednesday 18th June, 8:30 pm, at the Fringe Park. Come and be fascinated by the way she brings the canvas to life before your eyes!

About Laura

“My challenge is to make people conscious of the union of the cosmos we admire with our inner world.”

Laura Mesones is an interdisciplinary European artist, originally from Spain. She focused in art at an early age and she finished her studies of Fine Arts at the University of Salamanca, Spain, in 2009. Since then, she has been teacher of development of creativity as well as artist in interdisciplinary projects, where she has explored different means of expression: Painting, writing, dance, drama and singing. In her solo and group exhibitions, live painting performances, and her didactic methods, she always pursues the same goal: Involve the audience in an intense transformation experience.
