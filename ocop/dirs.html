The Directors

ALEX GELFAND

Producer, Script, Audio, Design

Born into theatre and music, Alex has been involved in local arts and performance since his earliest years, mostly as a prop or setting. A seasoned technician and observer of the stage, this is his own first ever theatrical production. His degrees in Music, Literature, History, and Classics have helped prepare him to create this tribute to the legends of story-telling, weaving many disparate elements into what he hopes will be a dreamy and memorable experience for all. Alex extends his deepest gratitude to everyone who helped make this play a reality, without whom none of this would be possible: My sincerest gratitude for your contribution of time, energy, and dedication to allow this play to physically exist for a brief moment in time. Thank you.


Roxane Hilary

Director, Set + Graphic Design

Roxane is a multi-disciplined student and professional of Theatre Tech.& Production and was able to apply so many of them to this project!

SOPHIA GRAZIANI

Costumes

Costume designer with a background in visual arts. A frequent collaborator with Montreal talent, I am so thrilled to be working on the costumes for Ocean Opera! My fortunate opportunities within the performing arts, recently includes working on Les Grands Ballets Canadiens 2014/2015 season campaign.

Enthusiastic about developing a unique approach to theatre design, I will be exploring the realm further in my studies at the National Theatre School of Canada in the Set and Costume Design program Fall 2014.
